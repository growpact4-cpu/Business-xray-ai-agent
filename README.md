# 🧠 Business X-Ray AI

> Reverse engineer any industry using AI. Analyze value chains, business models, competition, distribution networks, opportunities, risks, and market entry strategies.

## 📖 Overview

Business X-Ray AI is an AI-powered industry analysis workflow built with **n8n**.

The workflow accepts an **industry**, **country**, and optional **company names**, then generates a structured business report designed for entrepreneurs, investors, students, and business enthusiasts.

Instead of providing generic summaries, the workflow focuses on understanding **how an industry actually works**, including its value chain, distribution model, competitive landscape, and practical entry strategies.
